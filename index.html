Center for Renewable and Sustainable Energy Studies

Vacation Work July, 2022

While working with the CRSES I was tasked with drafting a pre-feasibility report for the installation of solar PV for a residential household.
Utilising software such as Homer Pro, I simulated various configurations to determine the optimal mixture of generation and storage capacities.
The household was then 3D modeled using PVsyst and the proposed system was tested to determine the effects of shading and the angle of installation.
